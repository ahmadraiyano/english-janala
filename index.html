Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes — this English learning website is available for users at no cost and you do not need to pay to access its content.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No — for this English learning web app, you do not need to create an account to use it. You can access the lessons and content without signing up or registering, and there’s no login or account creation required on the site itself.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your English vocabulary by regularly reading English content and noting new words, using flashcards or a notebook to review them, watching or listening to English media like movies or podcasts, and practicing the new words in your speaking and writing.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
No — this English learning site does not provide certificates for completing lessons. It’s a simple vocabulary/learning project without any certificate issuance feature.
